Vacation Planner Application

The Vacation Planner is an Android mobile application designed to help users organize and manage their vacations and excursions. Users can create, edit, and delete vacations and their associated excursions, set notification reminders, generate reports, and search through their data — all stored securely on the device using a local Room database.

Features

  • Secure login and registration with hashed password protection
  • Full vacation and excursion management (Create, Read, Update, Delete)
  • Real-time search for vacations and excursions
  • Vacation report generation with date-time stamp
  • Notification reminders for vacation and excursion dates
  • Share vacation details with other apps
  • Date validation ensuring excursions fall within vacation dates
  • Role-based user accounts (Admin and Regular User)
